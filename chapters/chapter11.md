### 3. Forms of Simple Present Verbs
- **Base form:** Used with subjects I, you, we, they.  
  - Example: We sometimes eat dessert.  
- **Verb + s:** Used with third-person singular subjects (he, she, it) and singular nouns.  
  - Example: He reads the newspaper.  
- **Special case â€“ Non-count nouns also take verb + s.**  
  - Example: Snow falls from the sky in winter.  
  - Example: My hair grows quickly.
